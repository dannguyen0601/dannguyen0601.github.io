About me

Hello everyone, I would like to introduce everyone about myself.
I called myself a dreamer who just wandering around to explore and find my own soul.
I am quite passionate in photography and art. I was a self-learner in paiting and photography.
I have my personal Instagram account where I share my photographic work, my thoughts and also my artwork.
To me, photography and art is not just entertainment activities, it reflects my soul, speak the voice that sometimes I cannot speak out loud.

My creativity was locked then. I forgot how much I love to create things. So I decided to quit.
Then I started to be a Freelancer, doing Content Marketing and also, I tried in a new field- Video Production as anAssistant Director and I realize I also passionate in Video Production.
Being an Assistant Director helps me to visualize about every position and their responsibility in the crew, to think more about what I really like and want to do more in the future. I want to be an Art Director and I will strengthen my skills and try my best to achieve my dream.
If not, I would like to be a Freelance artist
